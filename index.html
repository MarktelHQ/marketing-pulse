Attendance Guidelines

To get the most from every event — and to give back to the chapter — we ask all attendees to follow these guidelines:

  • 1 Share your notes. Post a summary or key takeaways in the chapter's shared channel within one week of attending.
  • 2 Capture actionable insights. Focus on what we can apply — frameworks, tools, strategies, or contacts relevant to our work.
  • 3 Present back to the chapter. For major conferences, prepare a short 10–15 minute lightning talk at the next chapter meetup.
  • 4 Connect and network. Actively engage with speakers and peers. Bring back at least one new connection or collaboration opportunity.
  • 5 Recommend or flag events. If you discover a great event not listed here, submit it so others can benefit too.
  • 6 Respect the budget. Prioritise events aligned with your development goals and discuss attendance with your line manager in advance.

About Marketing Pulse

Marketing Pulse is a curated events hub for marketing chapter professionals. We aggregate conferences, workshops, summits, and virtual events across Europe — giving you one place to discover opportunities for professional growth.

  • Weekly updates. Events are refreshed automatically every week through an AI-powered crawl of trusted sources.
  • European focus. Primarily covering events in the UK, DACH, Nordics, Benelux, France, Iberia, and broader European markets.
  • Development-first. We prioritise events that offer genuine learning — not just expos or trade shows.
  • Community-driven. Anyone in the chapter can submit an event they've found valuable or plan to attend.
  • Open and transparent. This is an experimental tool, continuously improved based on chapter feedback.
